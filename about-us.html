About Us

Who We Are

MAC (Mihama–Araya Company) is a joint venture established to provide reliable refrigerant supply and related HVAC solutions to OEMs, distributors, and the aftermarket in Thailand.

With refrigerants as our core business, MAC supports a wide range of customer needs through professional supply, logistics, and sourcing capabilities, serving the HVAC, refrigeration, and industrial markets with a focus on stability, quality, and long-term partnership.

Our Background & Joint Venture

MAC was formed through a partnership between Mihama Corporation (Japan) and Thailand-based companies Red Hills and RC Supply.

Mihama Corporation brings decades of experience in refrigerants, chemicals, and industrial solutions across Asia and global markets, operating with strong standards in quality, safety, and compliance. Together with Red Hills and RC Supply, which contribute deep local market knowledge, logistics capability, and customer networks in Thailand, MAC combines global-quality standards with local execution strength.

This joint venture structure enables MAC to support customers with both international credibility and responsive local service, delivering dependable supply and flexible sourcing tailored to market needs.
