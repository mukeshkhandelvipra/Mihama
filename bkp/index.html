Facts & Questions

Contact Us

Smart homes offer enhanced security, energy savings, convenience, and remote control of all devices. Studies show smart home users save up to 30% on energy bills annually while enjoying unprecedented comfort.

Installation costs vary based on the size of your home and devices chosen. We offer customized packages starting from $499 for basic setups to comprehensive whole-home solutions. Get a free estimate today!

Our smart home systems are largely self-maintaining. We provide OTA (over-the-air) software updates and a dedicated support line. Annual on-site checkups are included in our premium service plans.

We use end-to-end encryption, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. All devices meet NIST cybersecurity standards. Your data stays on your private network — we never sell or share it.
