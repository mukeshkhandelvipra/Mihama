About Us

Mihama Araya Company (MAC) is a joint venture between Mihama Corporation (Japan) and Thailand-based partners, providing reliable refrigerant supply and related HVAC solutions to OEMs, distributors, and the aftermarket in Thailand. Backed by global-quality standards and Japan-based expertise, MAC combines professional sourcing, local logistics, and long-term partnership to support customer supply needs.
